About Us

At AnimFX, we are passionate about all things related to animation and its tools and technologies. We are a community of individuals who love to create, learn, and share our knowledge about animation. Our club is dedicated to providing expert sessions on how to make animations using the latest tools and technologies. We aim to provide valuable insights and practical knowledge to aspiring animators and help them hone their skills.

What is AnimFX?

AnimFX is a club dedicated to all things related to animation and its tools and technologies. We organize expert sessions, workshops, seminars, screenings, and competitions to help our members learn, network, and showcase their skills.

What kind of animation does AnimFX cover?

AnimFX covers a wide range of animation techniques, including 2D, 3D, stop motion, and more. Our expert sessions and workshops focus on the latest tools and technologies used in these techniques.

What kind of events does AnimFX organize?

AnimFX organizes a variety of events, including expert sessions, workshops, seminars, screenings, and competitions. Our events cover a range of topics, from animation techniques and tools to career opportunities and industry trends.

How can I participate in AnimFX events?

You will receive invitations to our events. You can also follow us on social media to stay updated about our events and register for them.

Can I showcase my animation work at AnimFX events?

Yes, you can showcase your animation work at AnimFX events. We encourage our members to share their creations and provide them with opportunities to showcase their skills.
